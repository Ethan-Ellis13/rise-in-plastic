How Can I Reduce My Use of Plastic?

"There are many ways to reduce our daily plastic waste. Some examples are that we can use reuseable bags, use reuseable/compostable straws, use reusable water bottles, etc. We use plastic daily, and there are so many ways we can find more eco friendly options."
