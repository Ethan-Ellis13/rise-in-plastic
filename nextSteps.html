How Can I Reduce Our Use of Plastic?



Finding Where Do You Use Plastic
First thing you need to do to reduce your plastic use is to mark down the areas of your daily life where you use plastic. Did you use plastic when getting your grocries? Did you use plastic to seal your food? Or did you use plastic to carry a drink? Start thinking about your daily intake of plastic, and find alternatives that you can use to reduce your plastic use.

Using Alternatives to Plastic
There are many ways to reduce our daily plastic waste. Some examples are that we can use reuseable bags, use reuseable/compostable straws, use reusable water bottles, resuable "plastic" wrap, etc. We use plastic daily, and there are so many ways we can find more eco friendly options to our needs.

Recycling and Picking Up Trash
If you see a non reusable plastic on a sidewalk, please pick up and throw it away at the closest trash can. Our oceans are becoming filled with tons of plastic by the day; and the marine life living within the oceans are consuming the plastics and/or getting trapped in it.
